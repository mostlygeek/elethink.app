What is Elethink?

Elethink is an iOS app for discovering and practicing cognitive strategies to quickly improve a low mood. It is inspired by CBT (Cognitive Behavioral Therapy) techniques with tools for recording your moments, thoughts, and feelings. Developing a habit of recording your moments helps build insight into your behaviors and ultimately how to change them. Elethink makes building those habits quicker and easier.

How do I try it?

Elethink is available for free as a beta.

Our goal is to help people build the skills to quickly reduce the effects of negative and irrational thoughts. We're still learning what's effective in the app and what needs more work. To accomplish our goal we will build a community that will help create the most effective app for developing healthier thinking habits.

To join the beta you will need:

  1. Need an iPhone running iOS 15 or higher
  2. Install the beta from https://testflight.apple.com/join/CrPoi37t
  3. Join our Elethink Discord Community
  4. Go to the #beta-testing channel to ask questions and share feedback
  5. Introduce yourself in the #general channel (optional)

Important Things To Know

  • The community is not a substitute for a mental health professional. If you are considering harming yourself or suicide please contact crisis services right now.

Main Features

Record What Matters

Capture the essential details about how you're feeling right now

Elethink makes it easy to record the the details of your mood, your current thoughts and the situation you're currently in.

Each section provides an easy and intuitive way to quickly enter the data.

Measure your specific feelings and their intensity. Record the details of what is happening right now. Brain dump your thoughts quickly. Getting it out of your head and into the app is the first step in getting rid of a low mood.

Track All Your Moments

An unlimited history of your mood, moment by moment

Which situations and thoughts are creating your low moods? Elethink provides an unlimited history and makes it easy to identify the patterns of things that are bringing you down.

Eliminate Thinking Errors

Elethink is inspired by CBT and comes built in with eleven of the most common cognitive distortions. Simply scroll through the built in list and pick the ones distorting a thought.
